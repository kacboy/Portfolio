Get your feet wet

Be receptive and open to the knowledge of others. Don’t be afraid to try new strategies, techniques, or ideas if they have positive implications. Make sure to always keep learning, improving, and practicing.



Put your best foot forward

Just as you should be open to other’s ideas, also be willing to share your own. Don’t withhold your ideas and skills over a fear of getting shut down. If you think what you can offer is beneficial, bring it forward.



Stand your ground

Draw the line if you think something has gone awry or goes against what you stand for. Maintain a level of respect for others and try to find compromise if possible. Don’t participate in work that contradicts or compromises your own beliefs.
