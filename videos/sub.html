The SUB at SFU | Minecraft Build

This is a Minecraft creative build showcase video I built and filmed as the lead Multimedia Editor at The Peak. The SUB is a building that has been under construction for the better part of 4 years and is constantly getting delayed, so we thought we'd take a crack at completing it. My assistant and I were already big Minecraft players but I learned about Minecraft Replay Mod and was able to utilize my familiarity with other in-game replay tools to create the time-lapse shots. It was our most viewed video of the semester because of the zeitgeist around this building among students.
