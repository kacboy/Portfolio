MoleScope Lite

This was the first public-facing marketing video of MetaOptima’s latest product, MoleScope Lite. The product is a more budget and patient-friendly alternative to earlier MoleScope models. It provides patients with the ability to remotely and routinely submit their moles/spots for professional review. This video was created to demonstrate the product’s purpose and workflow to doctors who may want to include MoleScope into their practice. I was given a script and I created this video over several weeks and multiple rounds of feedback.
